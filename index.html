How To Play

The silhouette of a random Pokemon will appear on the image above, and four possible choices will be displayed.

Click or tap on the answer you think is correct. You'll get three chances to pick the right one, but you'll be awarded less points if you have to guess again!

Once you've picked the correct answer (or lost the round) a new silhouette for a different Pokemon will appear and you'll have the chance to pick from new choices.

After 10 rounds, your final score will be displayed. 50 points is a perfect game. Good luck!
